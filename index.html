YOTVATA FOR LIFE

The taste of Yotvata comes to you all the way from the kibbutz in the heart of the desert. The clean air, the peace and tranquility, the breathtaking scenery and the vast open spaces that embrace you with every sip and taste, bring that magical place all the way to you.

OUR PRODUCTS

Ice Coffee

Iced coffee milk is a refreshing blend of bold coffee and creamy milk, perfect for a cool caffeine boost. Enjoy it as a revitalizing pick-me-up or a delicious treat on a hot day.

Chocolate Milk

Chocolate milk is a classic favorite, combining the richness of chocolate with the creaminess of milk. This delicious treat is perfect for a quick energy boost or a sweet indulgence anytime.

Pistachio

Pistachio milk is a creamy, nutty alternative to dairy, rich in healthy fats, protein, and antioxidants. Its unique taste and subtle sweetness make it perfect for smoothies, coffee, or enjoyed on its own.
